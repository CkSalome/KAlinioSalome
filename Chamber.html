Tricks

1. Off angle Anchors Using your anchor, you can teleport pre-round to "off angles" or places that people don't normally expect another player to be at. Using this, you can suprise the enemy the moment they get in your vision. You can also just teleport away if you miss your opportunity.
2. Insta Bullet When using a gun that takes a long time to reload, some people prepare to immediately switch to their secondary gun after shooting. However, there is still a slight delay to when you can shoot when switching between the guns. Instead, if you switch to a headhunter, the delay when you can shoot is significantly less.
