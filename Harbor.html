Harbor

Real Name: Varun Batra "Varun Batra", also known as Harbor, is a Radiant from India who is the latest recruit of the VALORANT PROTOCOL so far. He has associations with REALM and has stolen artifacts. He seems to have the second gauntlet or arm piece that belongs to astra... the only difference being that he controls water.

Tricks

1. Peaceful cove Harbor's cove can block all bullets. It has 500hp before the shield breaks down. This is equivalent to 12 vandal bullets, the most common gun in the game. You can use this to defuse upto atleast half of the spike without that much risk anymore. Just beware of agent ability damage as they can pass through.
2. Tidal push Use your "High Tide for covering space quickly (note that bullets still go through the wave). The wave obstructs vision both vertically and still somewhat horizontally on the path directly infront of you."
