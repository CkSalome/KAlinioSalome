Phoenix
Real Name: Jamie Adeyemi "Phoenix", also known as Jamie Adeyemi, is a radiant from the UK. His powers specialize in flame related powers. He is one of the few agents to meet his omega counter-part. He is the 9th recruit of the VALORANT Protocol
Origin: United Kingdom
Race: Radiant
Gender: Male

Tricks

1. Wall flash Using the Blaze utility you can obstruct your enemy's vision and information on your team's standing. This makes it hard for them to push thru it. The flashes of Phoenix or the Curveball can go thru his walls, combined with the wall to obstruct vision you can do un unexpected flash and push thru without much consequences.
2. Phoenix heal While the Hot Hands and Blaze ability can damage enemies, it can actually also be used to heal himself as he is immune to his own fire damage. The Phoenix fire can make you heal upto 80hp with both abilities in a quick amount of time giving you an edge to re-engage enemies for multifrags.
