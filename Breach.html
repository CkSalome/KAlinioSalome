Breach

Real Name: Erik Torsten "Breach", is a Swedish recruit with robotic arms probably powered by radianite. His artificial arms create enough force to send shock waves through the ground. He is the 13th recruit of the Valorant Protocol.
Origin: Sweden
Race: Human
Gender: Male

Breach's Role and Abilities

Role:

 Initiator

Abilities

Aftershock


The abilities of agents under this role focus on initiating attacks on site, flushing enemies out of hiding spots and corners in order to assist the team when charging into battle.
Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
Description: EQUIP a fusion charge. FIRE the charge to set a slow-acting burst through the wall. The burst does heavy damage to anyone caught in its area.

Flashpoint

Costs: 250 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
Description: EQUIP a blinding charge. FIRE the charge to set a fast-acting burst through the wall. The charge detonates to blind all players looking at it.

Tricks

1. Clearing out Breach's utility is more for clearing corners than gathering information setting up the team. Clear close corners with your aftershock, clear a hallway or hall with your fault line, and clear wide spaces with your flashpoint.
2. Let's go A Breach's ultimate is best used when entering a site for an explosive entry. This is because it will concuss and stun a WHOLE area and give you free kills if backed-up by your other utility. Entering a site is also way easier using this ultimate.
