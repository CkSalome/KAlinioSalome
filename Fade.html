Tricks

1. Fade Haunt lineups An unexpected haunt can scan their line of sight, a well placed one can scan an entire area. Because haunt's can scan an entire area with no vertical limit, you can make lineups that take advantage of this. By throwing a haunt at the top of high places, you can almost always expect noone to destroy it at an instant (unless it is clearly seen from below). This is because people don't usually look up at the sky. The benefit of perfecting this is that when you find a perfect spot for it, it can scan an entire area and more.
2. Seize lineups A seize will force you into its center. Taking advantage of this, You can forcefully position enemies into a more dangerous position that can make it easier for you to kill them or to stop them from defusing the spike for a slight moment. You can also implement lineups that can let you use a seize a far away distance from the enemy.
