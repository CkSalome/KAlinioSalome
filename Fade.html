Tricks

1. Fade Haunt lineups An unexpected haunt can scan enemies, a well placed one can scan many. Thinking this you can create line ups for the fade haunt on top of things such as buildings or boxes because they have to flick to it and you wouldnt just casually look at the sky right?
2. Seize lineups A seize will force you into its center, taking advantage of this. You can put enemies into a more dangerous position out in the open or stopping them from defusing the spike. To boost efficiency and minimize risk you can create line ups for this.
