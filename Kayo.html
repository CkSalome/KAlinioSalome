Abilities

Frag/ment


The abilities of agents under this role focus on initiating attacks on site, flushing enemies out of hiding spots and corners in order to assist the team when charging into battle.
Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
Description: EQUIP an explosive fragment. FIRE to throw. The fragment sticks to the floor and explodes multiple times, dealing near lethal damage at the center with each explosion.

Flash/drive

Costs: 250 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
Description: EQUIP a flash grenade. FIRE to overhand throw. ALT FIRE to underhand throw a weaker version that explodes quickly. The flash grenade explodes after a short fuse, blinding anyone in line of sight.

Tricks

1. Frag/ment lineups a Frag/ment dishes out so much damage and can go through walls, this can also damage utilities. You can create line ups to get it on usual spots people plant spike on or use a killjoy ult on to destroy it.
2. One way pop flash. A kayo flash can bounce off walls as if its a grenade it has a trajectory of one it explodes after approximately 2s. Using this you can create one way instant pop flashes on window ways or above walls to set up your teammates.
