Yoru

Real Name: Ryo Kiritani "Yoru", Ryo Kiritani, is a Radiant from Tokyo, Japan with teleportation powers that can "rip the fabric of reality". He is the 15th recruit of the Valorant Protocol. He is generally dubbed by his fellow agents as "Rift walker" and appears to be loved by omen because of their similarity to rip the fabric of reality and teleport.
Origin: Japan
Race: Radiant

Tricks

1. Ground Pop Flash A yoru flash reaches higher above your head when you pop it to the ground. This can extremely help on one way flashing as the pop flash itself doesn't actually blind you at all.
2. Clone Picks A yoru clone is commonly not used to its full potential. Pre-setting your clone to the opposite side and peeking off of it can confuse your opponents on where you actually are. You can also place a clone right before exiting your ultimate to make them more likely to shoot the clone first due to the end-game situation usually being stressful. You can also use a clone as a blind and as a sound dampener for your gate crash to move to different spaces quickly without your opponents knowing.
