Sage

Real Name: Ling Ying Wei
Origin: China
Race: Radiant
Gender: Female


"Sage", also known as Ling Ying Wei, is a Radiant monk from China. She is the 7th recruit of the Valorant Protocol.


Sage's Role and Abilities


Role:

Sentinel

The abilities of a sentinel are mainly used to defend and support the team.

Abilities

Barrier Orb

Costs: 400 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
Description: EQUIP a barrier orb. FIRE places a wall that fortifies after a few seconds. ALT FIRE rotates the targeter. It has 400hp when placed and 800 when fully fortified.

Slow Orb

Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
Description: EQUIP a slowing orb. FIRE to throw a slowing orb forward that detonates upon landing, creating a lingering field that slows players caught inside of it. Slows the player down by 50% when on the field.

Healing Orb

Costs: 0 creds (Signature ability)
Max Charges per round: 1 (Recharges after 45 seconds after used)
Description: EQUIP a healing orb. FIRE with your crosshairs over a damaged ally to activate a heal-over-time on them. ALT FIRE while Sage is damaged to activate a self heal-over-time. Heals only hp and upto 60hp.

Ressurection

Costs: 8 ultimate points
Description: EQUIP a resurrection ability. FIRE with your crosshairs placed over a dead ally to begin resurrecting them. After a brief channel, the ally will be brought back to life with full health.

Tricks


1. "Grim" Wall

Using a Barrier Orb, you can jump on it to get elevation. In this example, the person placed the wall on a ledge so that the wall would float mid-air.


2. Double Ressurection

If you ressurect someone at the very start of the round, get all the ultimate orbs scattered in the map, get all kills, and plant the spike, it is theoretically possible to ressurect another person in the same round.
