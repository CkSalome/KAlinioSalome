"Omen", with a mysterious past, is a radiant from Earth with an unspoken origin Omen has teleportation powers that can "rip the fabric of reality". Whenever he teleports it is said that he feels pain in every time he does so. In his human past form he appears to be friends with "Viper" and when he dies it must be letting go of himself as he's a phantom and not solid matter...


Omen's Role and Abilities


Role:

Controller


Controllers are one of the four roles that agents are divided into in VALORANT. The abilities of agents under this role allow them to assist their teammates by cutting enemy vision, taking control of an area, and forcing enemies into choke points

Abilities

Shrouded step

Costs: 100 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
EQUIP a shrouded step ability and see its range indicator. FIRE to begin a brief channel, then teleport to the marked location.

Blindside

Costs: 300 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
EQUIP a blinding orb. FIRE to throw it forward, briefly reducing the vision range and deafening all players it touches. This projectile can pass through walls.

Gatecrash

Costs: 150 creds (Signiture ability)
Max Charges per round: 1 (Recharges after 30 seconds)
EQUIP a shadow orb, entering a phased world to place and target the orbs. PRESS the ability key to throw the shadow orb to the marked location, creating a long-lasting shadow sphere that blocks vision. HOLD FIRE while targeting to move the marker further away. HOLD SECONDARY FIRE while targeting to move the marker closer. PRESS RELOAD to toggle normal targeting view.

From the shadows

Costs: 7 ultimate points
EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to begin teleporting to the selected location. While teleporting, Omen will appear as a Shade that can be destroyed by an enemy to cancel his teleport, or press EQUIP for Omen to cancel his teleport.

Tricks


1. One way Smokes

The visual point of the eye is much greater when further from a wall. This can be exploited by putting a smoke on a wall on a door way of a hallway to obscure their eyevision while being able to see their legs. This gives you the advantage on defensive side but it's rarely used for attack or retake.


2. Ninja Entry

Due to omen's tp allowing him for different elevations, His tp can allow him to take off angles by tping up boxes or any other things. This can be further taken advantage of when smoking or double smoking obscuring enemies of the information making them more weary and paranoid everytime.
