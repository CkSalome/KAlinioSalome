Yoru

Real Name: Ryo Kiritani
Origin: Japan
Race: Radiant
Gender: Male


"Yoru", Ryo Kiritani, is a Radiant from Tokyo, Japan with teleportation powers that can "rip the fabric of reality". Hhe is the 15th recruit of the Valorant Protocol. He is generally dubbed by his fellow agents as "Rift walker" and appears to be loved by omen because of their similarity to rip the fabric of reality and teleport.


Yoru's Role and Abilities


Role:

Duelist


Agents under this role have abilities that allow them to focus on being the first to enter sites and engage the enemy.

Abilities

Fakeout

Costs: 100 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
EQUIP an echo that transforms into a mirror image of Yoru when activated. FIRE to instantly activate the mirror image and send it forward. ALT FIRE to place an inactive echo. USE to transform an inactive echo into a mirror image and send it forward. Mirror images explode in a blinding flash when destroyed by enemies.

Blindside

Costs: 250 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
EQUIP to rip an unstable dimensional fragment from reality. FIRE to throw the fragment, activating a flash that winds up once it collides with a hard surface in world.

Gatecrash

Costs: 200 creds (Signature ability)
Max Charges per round: 2 (Recharges after 2 kills)
EQUIP a rift tether. FIRE to send the tether forward. ALT FIRE to place a stationary tether. ACTIVATE to teleport to the tether's location. USE to trigger a fake teleport.

Dimensional Drift

Costs: 7 ultimate points
EQUIP a mask that can see between dimensions. FIRE to drift into Yoru's dimension, unable to be affected or seen by enemies from the outside.

Tricks


1. Ground Pop flash

A yoru flash reaches higher above your head when you pop it to the ground. This can extremely help on one way flashing when on your Ultimate as they can't see you so you can get close to them then pop out get free picks. Another way is waiting behind on radianite boxes as it's a perfect height for a one way flash just don't forget flashes are based on contact so even if you don't get flashed make sure to cover as much space for a flash.


2. Clone Picks

A yoru clone is commonly not used to its full potential. Pre setting your clone to the opposite side and peeking off of it can make your enemies blind or make it harder to aim for you as they need to flick. you can also do this going out of your ult to get a higher chance of a pick with less risk. generating sounds with your clone can misdirect your enemy on where you are as well making them doubt where you are next time you clone out.
