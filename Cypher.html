Tricks

1. Corner Cages Place your cage somewhere where you can hide on a corner. The enemies won't expect you to be there and you can sneak up behind them for easy kills.
2. One Way Cages The cypher cage has a limit on its vertical height. You can abuse this by placing it above an entranceway to where you can see the feet of your opponents on the other side but they cannot see you (which is exactly what "one-way" means). This can setup for easy kills.
