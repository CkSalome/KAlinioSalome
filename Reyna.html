Tricks

1. See the Leer When leering, it is recommended to place it on off-angle spots (spots that aren't usually prepared to focus on) like on the floor or high in the sky. This is because leers blind your enemies as long as they are within their vision and not when they are directly looking at it. Because the leer is placed in such a way, it will make it harder for your opponents to destroy it while focusing on you.
2. Deep Blind Since reyna's flash can go through walls you can set your teammates up with an early flash inside the area making it easier to entry.
