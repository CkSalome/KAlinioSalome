Tricks

1. Super Updraft There are certain spots on the maps in valorant where you could updraft and lift up jett 5x the normal updraft range for the cost of a single utility. This is very overpowered when combined with her ultimate because it will require people to change their crosshair placement and look up in the sky. (The example given is in the map "Icebox")
2. Smoke Dash Jett has smokes that lasts for 4 seconds. Making use of these as normal smokes just like how controllers do with theirs would be mostly a waste. Instead, you'd want to throw this smoke into the middle of the site and quickly dash into it. This would put the opponents on a difficult position as they need to take care of you and your teammates at the same time. This is one of the main methods used by people who play Jett to entry for their team and clear site that way.
