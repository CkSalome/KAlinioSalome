Jett

Real Name: Sunwoo Han
Origin: South Korea
Race: Radiant
Gender: Female


"Jett", also known as Sunwoo Han, is a Radiant from Korea with wind related powers who was a chef in her early life. She is the 10th recruit of the Valorant Protocol.


Jett's Role and Abilities


Role:

Duelist


Agents under this role have abilities that allow them to focus on being the first to enter sites and engage the enemy.

Abilities

Cloudburst

Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
INSTANTLY throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. HOLD the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair.

Updraft

Costs: 150 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
INSTANTLY propel Jett high into the air.

Tailwind

Costs: 0 creds (Signature ability)
Max Charges per round: 1 (Recharges after 2 kills)
ACTIVATE to prepare a gust of wind for a limited time. RE-USE the wind to propel Jett in the direction she is moving. If Jett is standing still, she propels forward.

Blade storm

Costs: 8 ultimate points
EQUIP a set of highly accurate throwing knives. FIRE to throw a single knife and recharge knives on a kill. ALTERNATE FIRE to throw all remaining daggers but does not recharge on a kill.

Tricks


1. Super Updraft

There are certain spots on the maps in valorant where you could updraft and lift up jett 5x the updraft range for the cost of a single utility. This is very over powered if combined with her ult because it will throw off people's crosshair placement while having a great aerial view of the map. (The super updraft is mainly in icebox)


2. Smoke Dash

Jett has smokes that lasts 4 seconds, making door way smokes for this would be mostly a waste. Instead you'd want to dash into a crucial part of a site or site entry way and look both sides while dashing to clear the corners, you can reangage after awhile creating space for your team.
