Tricks

1. Frag/ment lineups a Frag/ment acts as a throwable item that deals damage in a circle shaped area. This Frag/ment can kill if you stand there for the whole duration. What's worse is that it can also go through walls and can destroy enemy utilities. You can create line ups (a predetermined angle to hit a specific spot) to get it on usual spots. These are usually the places where people plant the spike on or places where people place their utility at, like a Killjoy ultimate.
2. One way pop flash. A Kay/o flash can bounce off walls just like a grenade. When throwing it normally (FIRE button), it takes around 2 seconds before it actually flashes the area. Using this, you can create a one way instant pop flash (a flash that activates almost immediately after the item was seen as well as only flash the enemy) on gaps and above walls to flash only the enemy and to set your teammates up for a free push.
