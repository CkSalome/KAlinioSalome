Brimstone

Real Name: Sabine Callas
Origin: America
Race: Human
Gender: Female


3 "Viper", also known as Sabine Callas, one of the leaders of the protocol, is a non Radiant from America. She is a mad scientist using her asenal poison powered inspired by the snake "viper".


Brimstone's Role and Abilities


Role:

Controller

Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.

Abilities

Snake bite

Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
EQUIP a chemical launcher. FIRE to launch a canister that shatters upon hitting the floor, creating a lingering chemical zone that damages and applies Vulnerable.

Poison Cloud

Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
EQUIP a gas emitter. FIRE to throw the emitter that perpetually remains throughout the round. ALT FIRE to lob. RE-USE the ability to create a toxic gas cloud at the cost of fuel. This ability can be RE-USED more than once and can be picked up to be REDEPLOYED.

Sky Smoke

Costs: 0 creds (Signature ability)
Max Charges per round: 1 (recharges every 2 seconds ATLEAST)
EQUIP a gas emitter launcher. FIRE to deploy a long line of gas emitters. RE-USE the ability to create a tall wall of toxic gas at the cost of fuel. This ability can be RE-USED more than once.

Viper's Pit

Costs: 7 ultimate points
EQUIP a chemical sprayer. FIRE to spray a chemical cloud in all directions around Viper, creating a large cloud that reduces the vision range of players and maximum health of enemies inside of it. HOLD the ability key to disperse the cloud early..

Tricks


1. Snake bite Line ups

The snake bite paired with the poison orb can decay your enemy to 1hp while finishing them off. The molly has same trajectory as a brimstone molly so learning brim line ups might be similar.


2. Shorty Ult

Using a shorty when in ult can have a good impact as staying longer in the ult can decay you to 1hp and the short visual makes it perfect for a shorty.
