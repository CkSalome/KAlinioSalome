"Raze", Tayane Alves, is a Human from Brazil with with an explosive style of gameplay. Her kit is generally regarded as an explosive entry or ones that are basically a run it down kit. She is the 12th agent of the Valorant Protocol. She is the energetic and chaotic type in the protocol and refers to the fellow agents as her family.


Raze's Role and Abilities


Role:

Duelist


Agents under this role have abilities that allow them to focus on being the first to enter sites and engage the enemy.
