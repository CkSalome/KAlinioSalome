Brimstone

Real Name: Liam Byrne
Origin: America
Race: Human
Gender: Male


"Brimstone", also known as Brimstone, sometimes called sarge, is a non Radiant from America who is the leader of the VALORANT Protocol. He is an ex military officer and a firefighter. He has an orbital arsenal letting him deploy smokes or a lazer from the sky.


Brimstone's Role and Abilities


Role:

Controller

Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.

Abilities

Stim Beacon

Costs: 200 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
INSTANTLY toss a stim beacon in front of Brimstone. Upon landing, the stim beacon will create a field that grants players RapidFire and a speed boost.

Incendiary

Costs: 250 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
EQUIP an incendiary grenade launcher. FIRE to launch a grenade that detonates as it comes to a rest on the floor, creating a lingering fire zone that damages players within the zone.

Sky Smoke

Costs: 0 creds (Signature ability) 100 for a charge each
Max Charges per round: 3 (2 of which are buyable)
EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to set locations where Brimstone's smoke clouds will land. ALTERNATE FIRE to confirm, launching long-lasting smoke clouds that block vision in the selected area.

Orbital strike

Costs: 7 ultimate points
EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to launch a lingering orbital strike laser at the selected location, dealing high damage-over-time to players caught in the selected area.

Tricks


1. Incendiary Line ups

The Incendiary is a molotov like ability that has a double bounce before landing. You can take this into a post plant to keep defusers away by making the ground of the spike unreachable because of the molotov damage. You could also sometimes just throw it in the air so that it has delay but it only works when the enemy has barely any time.


2. No smoke cover diffuse

A little fact about the orbital strike is that it does less damage at the sides of the circle. If you half the defuse and ult yourself on the side while defusing, the enemy will mostlikely be confused and won't try to swing you giving you a free half of the defuse.
