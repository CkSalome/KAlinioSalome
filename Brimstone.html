Brimstone

Real Name: Liam Byrne "Brimstone", also known as Brimstone, sometimes called sarge, is a non Radiant from America who is the leader of the VALORANT Protocol. He is an ex military officer and a firefighter. He has an orbital arsenal letting him deploy smokes or a lazer from the sky.
Origin: America
Race: Human
Gender: Male

Stim Beacon


Controllers are experts in covering up dangerous territory by placing "smokes" or "walls" to set their team up for success.

Max Charges per round: 1
Description: INSTANTLY toss a stim beacon in front of Brimstone. Upon landing, the stim beacon will create a field that grants players RapidFire and a speed boost.

Tricks

1. Incendiary Line ups The Incendiary is a molotov like ability that has a double bounce before landing. You can take this into a post plant to keep defusers away by making the ground of the spike unreachable because of the molotov area and it's damage. You cannot outlast the damage when defusing.
2. No smoke cover diffuse A little fact about the orbital strike is that it does less damage at the sides of the circle. If you half the defuse and ult yourself on the side while defusing, the enemy will most likely be confused and won't try to swing you giving you a free half of the defuse.
