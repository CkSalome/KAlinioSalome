Raze

Real Name: Tayane Alves "Raze", Tayane Alves, is a Human from Brazil with with an explosive style of gameplay. Her kit is generally regarded as an explosive entry or ones that are basically a run it down kit. She is the 12th agent of the Valorant Protocol. She is the energetic and chaotic type in the protocol and refers to the fellow agents as her family.
Origin: Brazil
Race: Human
Gender: Female

Tricks

1. Double satchel A double satchel can boost your movement a ton. it can make you traverse both vertically and horizontally a lot faster. Paired with the show stopper it is unstoppable.
2. Satchel + Show Stopper Instead of killing people using a show stopper, you can use it backwards to technically propel you a little more forwards. It is somewhat comparable to a Jett super updraft but instead covers horizontal distance. This can help when you need to cover a lot of distance while being flashy at the same time.
