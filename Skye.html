Tricks

1. Big Leap for dogkind The trailblazer, or "Skye dog", should and be mainly used to clear corners and give information. This however, has a time duration. So, we have to learn the best path possible so that we can use it to its fullest potential. When the time is running out, left click to make your dog leap and get an additional space which can sometimes win the round at tense moments.
2. Fake Flash At a highly skilled competitive game, whenever someone sees a flash, they try to look away from it as fast as they can. This is because most flashes' duration depends on how long you looked at the flash before it fully flashes you. Use this to an advantage by using a guiding light but not activating it immediately. Your opponents would expect a flash and look away. But instead, by delaying it, you can pop the flash the moment they look back for an easy kill on them.
