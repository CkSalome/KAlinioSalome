Tricks

1. One way Smokes The visual point of the eye is much greater when further from a wall. This can be exploited by putting a smoke on a wall on a door way of a hallway to obscure the opponent's vision while still being able to see them. This gives you a really big advantage as your opponents have no way to counter other than to retreat or to push towards you blindly.
2. Ninja Entry Due to omen's tp allowing him to travel in different elevations, His tp can allow him to take off angles by tping up boxes or any other things. This can be further taken advantage of when smoking or double smoking obscuring enemies of the information making them more weary and paranoid everytime.
