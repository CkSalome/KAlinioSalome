Sova
Real Name: Alexander (Sasha) Novikov "Sova", also known as Alexander
(Sasha) Novikov, is an archer
from the town of Severomorsk,
in Murmansk Oblast, Russia. He
is the 6th recruit of the Valorant
Protocol. His right eye was
replaced with a mechanical one.
He is mainly useful
for gathering information. He is
also very reliable. Sova has also
earned a reputation of being
one of the Protocol's most
trusted agents, especially
to the organization's
commander, Brimstone.
Origin: Russia
Race: Human
Gender: Male

Sova's Role and Abilities

Role:

 Initiator

Abilities

Owl Drone


The abilities of an Initiator are
mainly used to gather
information and to flush
people out of their positions.
Costs: 400 creds
Max Charges per round: 1
Description: EQUIP an owl drone. FIRE to deploy and take control of movement of the drone. While in control of the drone, FIRE to
shoot a marking dart. This dart will reveal the location of any player struck by the dart. The drone
can last upto 7 seconds and has 100hp.

Shock Bolt

Costs: 150 creds
Max Charges per round: 2
Description: EQUIP a bow with shock bolt. FIRE to send the explosive bolt forward, detonating upon collision and damaging players
nearby. HOLD FIRE to extend the range of the projectile. ALTERNATE FIRE to add up to two bounces to this arrow.
Each bolt can deal upto 75 damage.

Recon Bolt

Costs: 0 creds (Signature ability)
Max Charges: 1 (Recharges after 40 seconds after used)
Description: EQUIP a bow with recon bolt. FIRE to send the recon bolt forward, activating upon collision and revealing the location
of nearby enemies caught in the line of sight of the bolt. Enemies can destroy this bolt. HOLD FIRE to extend
the range of the projectile. ALTERNATE FIRE to add up to two bounces to this arrow.

Hunter's Fury

Costs: 8 ultimate points
Max Charges per round: (Ultimate)
Description: EQUIP a bow with three long-range, wall-piercing energy blasts. FIRE to release an energy blast in a line in front of Sova,
dealing damage and revealing the location of enemies caught in the line. This ability can be RE-USED up to two more
times while the ability timer is active. Each hit deals 80 damage.

Tricks

1. Recon Bolt Lineups With practice and research, a person can use the recon bolt to make it land
somewhere at a designated spot either near or even very far away to
gather player information. Like in the example, you shoot the recon bolt
at the position with the specific amount of charges and bounces.
2. Double Shock Bolt This is basically the same concept as trick 1. However, instead you shoot 2
bolts that should land at the same spot AT THE SAME TIME. If pulled
correctly, you can deal upto 150 damage which is an instakill even with shields on.
