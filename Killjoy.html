Tricks

1. Nano Alarm Place your alarm bot over nanoswarms to set a deadly trap for enemies to cross over. This area not only alarms you of the enemies' position but also lets you activate nanoswarms that can deal massive damage.
2. Turret Holding Let your turret hold majority of the area when you are supposed to defend a spot. This takes away the risk required when trying to stall and await enemies that approach you. The turret can also work as an alarm bot that even points and shoots towards any enemy it sees in its vision. It leaves the enemies in a tough situation as they need to cover and clear areas which also loses a lot of time.
